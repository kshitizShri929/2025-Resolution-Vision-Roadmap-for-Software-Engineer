# 2025 Resolution & Vision Roadmap for Chandan - Software Engineer

### 2025 Resolution:
By the end of 2025, I aim to become a proficient Software Engineer with a salary range of 6-10 LPA, mastering backend technologies, improving my communication skills, and building my confidence. My journey will focus on technical excellence, recognition in the tech community, and financial stability.

### SMART Goals for 2025:

#### Specific:
- **Technical Skills:** Become an expert in Java, Spring Boot, and databases like MySQL. Focus on mastering advanced coding concepts, algorithms, and system design.
- **Communication Skills:** Develop my verbal and written communication, ensuring that I can confidently participate in interviews and presentations.
- **Time Management:** Organize my daily routine to balance work, learning, health, and personal development efficiently.
- **Physical Health:** Incorporate regular exercise and yoga into my daily schedule to maintain overall well-being.
- **Salary Goal:** Aim for a salary between 6-10 LPA by the end of 2025.

#### Measurable:
- Complete 2 advanced technical projects or certifications in Java, Spring Boot, or full-stack development.
- Participate in at least 3 coding competitions like Hackathons, Codeforces, or LeetCode.
- Take 3 communication courses and apply them in real-life scenarios like interviews.
- Exercise 4-5 times a week for 30 minutes and track overall health improvements.
- Publish at least 5 technical blogs or contribute to open-source projects.

#### Achievable:
- Dedicate 2 hours daily to enhance technical skills, aiming for one major project each quarter.
- Spend 1 hour daily improving communication skills through online courses and practice.
- Set aside time for physical and mental health (exercise, yoga, and meditation).
- Regularly evaluate progress, seeking feedback from mentors or peers.

#### Relevant:
- All goals contribute to my success as a software engineer, focusing on building my skills, confidence, and financial stability.

#### Time-bound:
- **End of Q1 (March 2025):** Complete one technical certification or course (Java, Spring Boot, or Data Structures/Algorithms).
- **End of Q2 (June 2025):** Complete two major projects and start participating in coding competitions.
- **End of Q3 (September 2025):** Improve communication skills and personal brand, prepare for job interviews.
- **End of Q4 (December 2025):** Secure a job or freelance position with a salary of 6-10 LPA, showcasing significant progress in both technical and soft skills.
