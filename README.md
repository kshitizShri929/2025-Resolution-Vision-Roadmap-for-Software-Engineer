# 2025 Resolution & Vision Roadmap for Software Engineer

### 2025 Resolution:
To become a skilled Software Engineer with a salary of 6-10 LPA, with improved technical expertise, communication skills, and overall confidence, working towards recognition, respect, and financial stability by the end of 2025.

### SMART Goals for 2025:

#### Specific:
- **Technical Skills:** Master backend technologies (Java, Spring Boot, and databases like MySQL) and advanced concepts in coding, algorithms, and system design.
- **Communication Skills:** Focus on improving both verbal and written communication to be more confident during interviews and presentations.
- **Time Management:** Create a structured schedule to balance work, learning, health, and personal development.
- **Physical Health:** Incorporate regular exercise and yoga into my daily routine to maintain mental and physical well-being.
- **Salary Goal:** Aim for a salary range of 6-10 LPA by the end of 2025.

#### Measurable:
- Complete 2 advanced projects or certifications in Java, Spring Boot, or full-stack development.
- Participate in at least 3 coding competitions (Hackathons, Codeforces, or LeetCode).
- Take 3 communication courses and apply the skills in interviews or discussions.
- Exercise for at least 30 minutes 4-5 times a week and track improvement in overall health.
- Work on at least 5 technical blogs or public projects to build a presence in the tech community.

#### Achievable:
- Dedicate 2 hours daily to learning technical skills, with a goal of completing one major project per quarter.
- Dedicate 1 hour daily to improving communication skills through courses and practicing with peers or mentors.
- Balance technical learning with health and self-care (yoga, meditation).
- Regularly evaluate progress and seek feedback from mentors or peers.

#### Relevant:
- All goals align with becoming a successful software engineer, securing a 6-10 LPA salary, and improving life satisfaction.

#### Time-bound:
- **End of Q1 (March 2025):** Complete one major technical certification or course (Java, Spring Boot, or Data Structures/Algorithms).
- **End of Q2 (June 2025):** Complete two advanced projects and start participating in hackathons or coding competitions.
- **End of Q3 (September 2025):** Reach an intermediate to advanced level in communication, interview preparedness, and personal brand building.
- **End of Q4 (December 2025):** Secure a job or freelance position with a salary of 6-10 LPA, achieving significant growth in technical and soft skills.

---

### Vision for 2025 (End Year):
By the end of 2025, I envision myself as a competent Software Engineer with a strong foundation in backend development (Java, Spring Boot, databases). I will have significantly improved my communication skills, making me confident in interviews and meetings. I will have a respectable salary between 6-10 LPA and have made strides toward gaining recognition in the tech community. I will be financially stable, living a balanced life with health, continuous learning, and personal growth.
